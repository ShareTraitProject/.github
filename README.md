# 🌱 ShareTrait
**Towards interoperable and reusable trait data for ectotherms**

---

## 🔍 What is ShareTrait?

**ShareTrait** is an open research initiative that streamlines the collection, standardisation, and reuse of **individual‑level species trait data** for ectotherms.

The project initally focuses on three core traits* that are fundamental to organismal fitness and ecological responses:
- **Metabolic rate**
- **Development time**
- **Fecundity**

 * efforts are underways to expand (but more traits are underways)
 
To understand how species respond and interact with their environment, more and more data are being collected on species traits. Despite the abundance of trait data, synthesis across studies is often limited by inconsistent formats, missing metadata, and a lack of interoperability. ShareTrait addresses these challenges by providing **FAIR‑compliant pipelines, curated datasets, and a relational database** that enable scalable, cross‑study analyses.

---

## 🎯 Vision and Goals

Our long‑term vision is to enable **large‑scale ecological, evolutionary, and physiological synthesis** by making trait data FAIR

Specifically, ShareTrait aims to:

- Lower barriers to data sharing  
- Reduce duplication of effort  
- Preserve rich metadata
- provide curated datasets
- Support transparent, open, and collaborative science  

---

## 📦 What does ShareTrait provide?

- **ShareTraitDatabase** – a relational SQL implementation of the ShareTrait data model
- **Positional paper** - highlighting the vision of ShareTrait: [read paper](https://doi.org/10.1111/1365-2435.70147)
- **Standardized Data Pipelines** - data reformatting, phylogenetic tools, facilitation attribution to primary data sources
